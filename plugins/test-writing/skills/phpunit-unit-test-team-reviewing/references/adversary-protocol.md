# Adversary Protocol

## Defense Round Rules

Rules for original reviewers responding to adversary challenges in Phase 7.

1. **"I already conceded" is not a defense** — if an adversary resurrects a finding you withdrew in round 1, engage with their argument on its merits. Your round 1 concession doesn't bind you. Reconsider.

2. **New findings get full treatment** — adversary-introduced findings must be challenged or conceded, same as round 1 peer findings.

3. **You may change your mind in either direction** — re-adopt a finding you previously withdrew, or withdraw a finding you previously defended, based on the adversary's argument.

4. **Round 2 final stance is binding** — replaces round 1 final stance as input to consensus merge.
